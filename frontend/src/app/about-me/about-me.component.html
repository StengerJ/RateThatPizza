About PGH-Pizza

PGH-Pizza is a Pittsburgh pizza tracking project maintained by Joshua Stenger.

A Pittsburgh pizza habit

In college my roommate Tema and I (Joshua Stenger) developed a habit of trying new pizza throughout the city of Pittsburgh. We've always wanted to catalog the pizza and track where we have been, and this is an amazing way to do it and potentially get the community of Pittsburgh involved as well!

- Joshua Stenger
